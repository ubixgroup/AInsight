Health Canada has developed a technical document to provide regulatory authorities and decision makers with an objective for the level of iron substances in Canadian drinking water supplies. We're looking for comments on this draft document from regulatory authorities, decision makers and the public. Who was the focus of this consultation We engaged with: the general public industry stakeholders academic communities non-governmental organizations federal, provincial and territorial regulatory and health protection agencies Key questions for discussion Ideas and input were sought around 3 topics: the proposed objective the approach used to develop it the potential impacts of implementation The input that was gathered through this process will be used to revise the document as needed.
