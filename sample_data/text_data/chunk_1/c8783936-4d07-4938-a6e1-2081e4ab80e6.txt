Subscribe to Food Additives e-Notice To subscribe, submit an email to foodadditives-subscribe-request@list.hc-sc.gc.ca with the email subject containing only the text "Subscribe". You will receive a confirmation e-mail from Food Additives e-Notice. You must reply to this confirmation e-mail in order to activate your e-Notice subscription. Cancel your Food Additives e-Notice Subscription If you are already a subscriber and wish to cancel, submit an email to foodadditives-unsubscribe-request@list.hc-sc.gc.ca with the email subject containing only the text "Unsubscribe". You will receive a confirmation e-mail from Food Additives e-Notice. You must reply to this confirmation e-mail in order to cancel your e-Notice subscription. Note: If you're having problems subscribing or unsubscribing, contact bcs-bipc@hc-sc.gc.ca.
